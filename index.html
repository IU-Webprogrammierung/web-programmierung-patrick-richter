BB is a graphic designer and art director based in Hamburg with a focus on editorial design, corporate identity and UX/design.

Her work is characterised by a clear and visually reduced design language, which is defined by the targeted use of colours and fonts.

Taking into account concept and content, based on the client‘s values and wishes. With a deep understanding of a holistic design approach that seamlessly integrates analogue and digital media to create a consistent look and feel.

For the realisation of more extensive projects, I work together with my freelance network of designers in Berlin and Hamburg.
